Starting my day

Recently i have been trying to wake up early and meditate.

I usually wake up at 9 am and have my breakfast at 10 o'clock. Before I eat I like to meditate and do yoga for 20 minutes.

For my breakfast I usually cook healthy meals full of proteins and fibers, which help me build my muscle strength. Tea is my essential morning drink, that helps me with my stomach. After my breakfast, I exercise for 30 minutes or one hour.

This pretty much sums up my mornings this summer. I hope that soon I will change my routine by going on a vacation.
